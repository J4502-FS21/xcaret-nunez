Welcome!

I am a senior-year journalism student at the University of Missouri—Columbia. From Yuma, Arizona, I came to MU and have gained training in all aspects of multimedia journalism with an emphasis in Radio-Television Reporting and Producing. This website showcases the journalistic work I have created and produced during my time at MU so far (2018-2021). The notable projects displayed throughout the site demonstrate my knowledge and technique in reporting and producing audio, video and photo stories. It also demonstrates my skills and familiarity with various broadcast equipment and programs such as Adobe Audition, Premiere Pro and Photoshop. In my spare time, I enjoy producing and broadcasting my own radio show at MU's college radio station, KCOU 88.1 FM, keeping up with my family back at home, and spending too much time scrolling through my Twitter feed while listening to the latest NPR Tiny Desk performance.
